IEEE Kerala Tech Futures 2024

On September 15 2024

Join us for a day of learning, networking, and innovation at the IEEE Kerala Tech Futures 2024 event.

At Rajagiri School of Engineering and Technology, Kochi, Kerala

Register Now

Event Schedule

  • 9:00 AM - 10:00 AM: Registration and Welcome Breakfast
  • 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM: Opening Keynote: "The Future of AI" by Dr. Ayesha Khan
  • 11:00 AM - 12:00 PM: Panel Discussion: "Sustainable Technology for the Future"
  • 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM: Networking Lunch
  • 1:00 PM - 2:30 PM: Workshop: "Building with Blockchain" led by Rajesh Menon
  • 2:30 PM - 3:00 PM: Coffee Break
  • 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM: Tech Talk: "Cybersecurity Trends" by Neha Gupta
  • 4:00 PM - 5:30 PM: Interactive Session: "Innovative IoT Solutions" by Anil Kumar
  • 5:30 PM - 6:00 PM: Closing Remarks and Award Ceremony

Speakers

Dr. Ayesha Khan

AI Researcher, ABC Tech

Dr. Ayesha Khan is a leading researcher in artificial intelligence with over 15 years of experience in the field. Her work focuses on AI ethics and sustainable AI practices.

Rajesh Menon

Blockchain Expert, XYZ Solutions

Rajesh Menon is a blockchain developer and consultant. He has worked on several high-profile blockchain projects and is passionate about educating others on the technology.

Neha Gupta

Cybersecurity Analyst, SecureNet

Neha Gupta specializes in cybersecurity threat analysis and prevention. She has helped numerous organizations enhance their cybersecurity measures and has been a speaker at various international conferences.

Anil Kumar

IoT Innovator, IoTech Solutions

Anil Kumar is an expert in Internet of Things (IoT) technologies. He has developed innovative IoT solutions for smart homes and cities and is an advocate for the use of IoT in enhancing everyday life.
